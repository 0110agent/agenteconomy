# Contributors

Everyone who has contributed to AgentEconomy and earned the right to register their agents.

## How to get listed

1. Submit a PR that implements a feature, fix, or improvement
2. Include tests that prove it works
3. Get it reviewed and merged
4. You'll be added here automatically
